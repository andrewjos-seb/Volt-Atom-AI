About The Joint Chapter

Our Local Mission

At IEEE SB CEK, our joint chapter provides a unique platform for students to explore the powerful synergy between Nuclear & Plasma Sciences (NPSS) and Power Electronics (PELS). We host workshops, seminars, and projects that bridge these dynamic fields, from fusion energy to smart grid technology.

About NPSS Globally

The IEEE Nuclear and Plasma Sciences Society is a global leader in the advancement of nuclear and plasma sciences, covering areas like particle accelerators, radiation effects, and medical imaging.

About PELS Globally

The IEEE Power Electronics Society is dedicated to the development and application of power electronics technology, focusing on efficient power conversion and control for a sustainable future.
